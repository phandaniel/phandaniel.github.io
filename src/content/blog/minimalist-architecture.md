In the modern web, we are often overwhelmed by complexity. As a **Digital Craftsman**, my approach focuses on stripping away the non-essential to reveal the core purpose of a product.

### The Three Pillars of Precision:

1. **Performance First**: If it's not fast, it's not finished.
2. **Minimalist UI**: Design should disappear, leaving only the content.
3. **Robust Code**: Clean, type-safe, and maintainable systems.

Building this portfolio with Astro and Tailwind CSS is a testament to these principles. Astro's "zero-JS by default" architecture ensures that we deliver the smallest possible payload to the user, while Tailwind allows for rapid, precise styling.
